System Details

Why

Modern workplaces need smarter tools to optimize productivity, ensure accountability, and better manage time spent at desks or in meetings. Traditional systems often fail to provide accurate, real-time data about whether an employee is actively working or idle. This system fills that gap by offering automated, AI-powered monitoring for real-time workplace engagement.

What

A real-time monitoring system that uses computer vision and AI to:

  • Detect individuals at office desks
  • Analyze their behavior (e.g., looking at laptop, facing camera)
  • Track working vs idle time
  • Record time spent in different zones (e.g., desk, breakroom, meeting room)
  • Provide detailed productivity reports

Who

  • Employers and managers who want to understand and optimize employee productivity
  • HR teams for performance tracking
  • IT/Admin departments for facility usage and zone management
  • Employees looking to improve their self-discipline and focus through visibility

When

  • During working hours, to monitor engagement and break durations
  • Throughout the workday to maintain a log of productive time vs idle time
  • Especially useful in remote/hybrid work environments and co-working spaces

Where

  • At office desks
  • In meeting rooms
  • In school classrooms or study areas
  • Across zoned office layouts (using cameras or edge devices)

How

  • Using camera-based computer vision models
  • Person detection (e.g., YOLO, OpenCV, or Deep Learning frameworks)
  • Behavior analysis based on head position, eye gaze, and proximity to the laptop
  • Time tracking algorithms to measure time spent in each zone
  • Optional integration with dashboards for analytics and reporting
